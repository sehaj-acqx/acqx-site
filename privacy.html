1. Who we are

acqX is an Australian marketing business that provides done-for-you client acquisition services - including lead generation, paid advertising and campaign management - for home renovation, floor-coating and trade businesses. This policy applies to the acqX website and all interactions with our business.

2. Information we collect

We only collect personal information that is reasonably necessary for our business functions. This may include:

  • Contact details - your name, email address, phone number and business name, provided when you submit an enquiry, book a call, or communicate with us.
  • Business information - details about your company, services, revenue range and marketing goals that you choose to share with us.
  • Technical & usage data - IP address, browser type, device information, referring pages and interactions with our website, collected automatically through cookies and similar technologies.
  • Advertising data - anonymised event and conversion data collected through advertising and analytics tools (such as the Meta Pixel and Google Analytics) to measure and improve campaign performance.

3. How we collect it

We collect personal information directly from you when you complete a form, book a strategy call, email or message us, or engage our services. We also collect technical data automatically when you use our website. Where you interact with our advertisements on third-party platforms (such as Facebook, Instagram or Google), those platforms may share limited data with us in accordance with their own policies.

4. How we use your information

We use personal information to:

  • provide, manage and improve our services;
  • send you information, proposals and service updates you have requested;

We do not sell your personal information. We will only use your information for direct marketing where we have your consent or another lawful basis, and you may opt out at any time.

We take reasonable steps to protect personal information from misuse, interference, loss, and unauthorised access, modification or disclosure. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we work to safeguard the information entrusted to us and to limit access to it.

8. Retention

We keep personal information only for as long as it is needed for the purposes described in this policy, or as required to meet legal, accounting or reporting obligations. When it is no longer required, we take reasonable steps to destroy or de-identify it.
